About

Hi, I’m Arron, a student of Information Systems at Universitas Pelita Harapan (UPH), intake 2023. While my academic focus is on technology and systems, I am passionate about UI/UX design and digital branding. I’ve gained hands-on experience creating application prototypes, which has fueled my interest in user-centered design and problem-solving through technology.

I believe UI/UX design is more than just visuals—it’s about crafting solutions that enhance usability and create meaningful experiences. Though still developing my skills, I’m excited to explore how creativity and technology can work together to leave a lasting impact.

As I grow, I aim to refine my design abilities, dive deeper into usability testing, and contribute to projects that make a difference in people’s digital journeys.
